# FIFA World Cup 2026 — Full Match Schedule

> **Source of truth for WC26 BETS.** All 104 matches across USA / Canada / Mexico.
> **Times are US Eastern (ET / EDT, UTC−4).** Each venue's local time differs — see the
> venue key. Tournament runs **June 11 – July 19, 2026**.
>
> Compiled from Al Jazeera, worldcupwiki, FIFA.com and Wikipedia (June 2026). Group-stage
> matchups are final (draw was Dec 5, 2025). Knockout matchups depend on group results, so
> they're shown as bracket placeholders. Kickoff times can still be adjusted by FIFA —
> reconfirm closer to each date.

## Format

- **48 teams → 12 groups (A–L) of 4.** Each team plays 3 group matches.
- **Group stage:** Jun 11–27. Top 2 of each group + 8 best third-placed teams advance (32 total).
- **Knockout:** Round of 32 (new this edition) → R16 → QF → SF → 3rd place → Final.

## Notes for WC26 BETS

- Times above are **ET**; the app stores dates as strings like `'Jun 11'` and `NextMatchBanner`
  parses against year **2026** — to enable accurate countdowns, consider storing an ISO datetime
  (date + ET kickoff) per match instead of a bare day string.
- Group-stage match IDs in `worldcup.js` (`A1`–`L6`) map to the matchups in the **Groups & teams**
  + matchday tables above. Knockout IDs (`R32_1..16`, `R16_1..8`, `QF1..4`, `SF1..2`, `3RD`,
  `FINAL`) correspond to matches **73→104** in order.
- Knockout matchups are placeholders until group results are known — exactly what the app's
  `homeDesc`/`awayDesc` + admin-assigned `knockoutTeams` flow is built for.
